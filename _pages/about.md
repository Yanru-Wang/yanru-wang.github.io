## About

Wei-Kun Chen is an Associate Professor in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Beijing Institute of Technology. His research focuses on mixed integer linear programming, especially theory, algorithms and optimization software, with applications in supply chain management and wireless communication.

## Research Interests

- Mixed integer linear programming
- Integer programming theory
- Algorithms and optimization software
- Supply chain management applications
- Wireless communication and network optimization
